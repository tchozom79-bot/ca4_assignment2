Need help? We’ve got answers.

Everything you need to know about dining at EAT ELYSIUM in one place.

How do I make a reservation?

Reservations can be made online through our reservation page or by calling us directly. We recommend booking in advance for weekends and special events.

Do you offer vegetarian and vegan dishes?

Yes, our menu includes a wide range of vegetarian and vegan options. Please let us know of any dietary preferences when reserving so we can prepare your meal with care.

Can I request a private dining area?

Private dining requests are welcome for celebrations and meetings. Contact us in advance and we’ll help arrange the best seating option for your group.

What are your opening hours?

We are open daily for lunch and dinner. Please visit our contact page for exact hours and holiday schedules.

Is parking available nearby?

Yes, guest parking is available close to the restaurant. If you need detailed directions, our team can send parking information with your reservation confirmation.

Do you accommodate food allergies?

Absolutely. Please tell us about any allergies or intolerances when you book, and our chefs will tailor a safe and delicious experience for you.

Didn't find your question?

Reach out directly and our team will respond quickly to help with special requests, menu details, or reservation changes.
